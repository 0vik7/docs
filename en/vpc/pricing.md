## Public IP addresses

In [!KEYREF vpc-short-name], you pay per hour of using public IP addresses. The minimum billing unit is one minute.

| Service | Cost of 1 hour, without VAT | Cost of 1 hour, with VAT |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Public IP address | 0.127 ₽ | 0.15 ₽ |
