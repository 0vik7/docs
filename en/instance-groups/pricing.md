# Pricing policy for [!KEYREF instance-groups-name]

[!KEYREF instance-groups-name] can be used free of charge. You can create instance groups and use the storage and computing resources within the [available limits](concepts/limits.md).

All other Yandex.Cloud services, such as creation of VMs or allocation of external IP addresses, are [charged as usual](../billing/pricing.md). Outgoing traffic is [charged](../compute/pricing.md#prices-traffic) similarly to other services.
