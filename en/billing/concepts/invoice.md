# Invoice

An invoice is a tax document that is issued by Yandex under the Tax Code of the Russian Federation.

## Invoice creation {#create-invoice}

[A report](../concepts/act.md) for services rendered and an invoice are generated at the end of the billing period for the amount actually used from the [personal account balance](../concepts/personal-account.md#balance).
